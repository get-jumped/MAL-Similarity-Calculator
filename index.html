MAL Similarity Calculator

Find what connects (and divides) your watchlists

Find similar and unique anime between MAL users.

Enter two or more MyAnimeList usernames to instantly compare their anime libraries. See which series you've both watched, what only one of you has seen, and how your ratings stack up on shared titles. Great for finding your next watch based on what a friend loved — or discovering how different your tastes really are.

Features:
  • Side-by-side comparison of two or more MAL profiles
  • Shared anime with users' scores highlighted
  • Unique titles each user has that the other hasn't seen yet
  • Filter by status (completed, watching, plan to watch, dropped)
